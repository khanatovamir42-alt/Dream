# 🌱 Dream — Turn Distraction Into Progress

**Dream** is a premium, AI-powered personal growth platform designed to help users replace mindless scrolling with meaningful progress toward their most ambitious goals.

Unlike traditional app blockers, **Dream** doesn't just stop you from using distractions; it provides a more compelling alternative by gamifying your personal growth and offering real-time AI coaching.

---

## ✨ Key Features

- 🛤️ **Dynamic Learning Paths**: Duolingo-inspired progression system that curves and adapts to your journey.
- 🤖 **AI Dream Coach**: A persistent, intelligent companion ready to guide, motivate, and teach (Ready for AI Integration).
- 🌳 **Dream Tree**: A visual representation of your growth. Watch your tree evolve from a seed to a forest as you complete lessons.
- 🧠 **Smart Interventions**: Beautiful, psychological nudges that trigger when you attempt to access distracting apps.
- 📈 **Skill Graph**: Visualize your mastery across different sub-skills related to your goal.
- 🛡️ **Privacy-First**: All your data is stored locally in your browser.

---

## 🏗️ Architecture

Dream is built as a **Modern SPA (Single Page Application)** using:
- **Frontend**: Vanilla JavaScript (ES6 Modules), HTML5, CSS3.
- **Design System**: Custom Glassmorphism UI, Fluid Animations.
- **Storage**: LocalStorage Persistence.
- **AI Ready**: Decoupled Provider architecture (OpenAI, Anthropic, Gemini compatible).
